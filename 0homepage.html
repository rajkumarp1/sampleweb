"Faded" is a song by English-Norwegian record producer and DJ Alan Walker with vocals provided by Norwegian singer Iselin Solheim. The single was originally set to be released on 25 November 2015, but was postponed to 3 December. The song was highly successful, peaking in the top 10 in most of the countries. It charted in, and reached the top spot in more than 10 countries. As of 25 February 2021, it is the 19th most viewed video on YouTube, with over 3 billion views, making it the first EDM track to hit that milestone. "Faded" is also the 41st most streamed song on Spotify as of February 2021, with over 1.3 billion streams.
